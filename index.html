Safety Measures

Earthquake Safety

  • Drop, cover, and hold on
  • Stay away from windows and heavy objects
  • If outdoors, move to an open area
  • Be prepared for aftershocks

Flood Safety

  • Move to higher ground immediately
  • Do not walk or drive through flood waters
  • Prepare an emergency kit
  • Follow evacuation orders promptly

Fire Safety

  • Install smoke alarms on every level
  • Create and practice a fire escape plan
  • Keep flammable items away from heat sources
  • Never smoke in bed

Hurricane Safety

  • Board up windows and secure outdoor items
  • Have a hurricane emergency kit ready
  • Follow evacuation orders if given
  • Stay indoors during the hurricane

Tornado Safety

  • Go to a basement or interior room on the lowest floor
  • Stay away from windows and outside walls
  • Use your arms to protect your head and neck
  • Have a battery-powered weather radio

General Emergency Preparedness

  • Create a family emergency plan
  • Build an emergency kit with essentials
  • Stay informed about local emergency protocols
  • Learn basic first aid and CPR
